"How much does it cost?" is the first question every operator asks — and the hardest to answer with a single number, because pricing is almost always custom. What you *can* do is understand the cost *model* and the ranges, so you're not blindsided. Here's an honest breakdown.

## Why quotes vary so wildly

Search around and you'll see white-label MVPs quoted anywhere from ~$8,000 to $150,000+ ([B2Broker](https://b2broker.com/news/white-label-crypto-exchange-cost/), [BTSE Solutions](https://btsesolutions.com/articles/white-label-crypto-exchange-cost)). That's not sources contradicting each other — it's a reflection of how different "a crypto exchange" can mean, from a spot MVP on a template to a multi-region derivatives venue with deep liquidity and high-availability infrastructure.

<Callout title="Read quotes carefully">
A low headline setup fee often excludes the things that actually matter — liquidity, compliance tooling, custom features — and SaaS providers frequently charge extra for anything outside their template, which industry breakdowns note can push real costs well above the initial estimate. Always price the *total*, not the sticker.
</Callout>

## The cost model (what you're actually paying for)

White-label pricing typically has three layers:

| Layer | What it covers | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Setup / integration fee** | Deployment, branding, integration | One-time; scales with customization |
| **Monthly platform / revenue share** | Ongoing licence, often a share of fees | Reported roughly $2,000–$15,000/mo by tier ([B2Broker](https://b2broker.com/news/white-label-crypto-exchange-cost/)) |
| **Operational costs** | Hosting, KYC (~$0.50–$2/user), banking, support | The line founders most often underestimate |

By contrast, **building from scratch** is a different order of magnitude — reported at several hundred thousand to well over a million dollars, plus 12–18 months ([Codono](https://codono.com/blog/white-label-crypto-exchange-vs-custom-build)). We compare the two directly in [White-Label vs. Building](/blog/white-label-vs-building-crypto-exchange).

## What to budget beyond the platform

- **Licensing & legal** — often the largest and slowest line; see [licensing](/blog/crypto-derivatives-licensing-global-guide).
- **Compliance tooling** — KYC/AML, sanctions screening, Travel Rule ([KYC/AML](/blog/kyc-aml-for-crypto-exchanges)).
- **Marketing & incentives** — maker rebates and acquisition to actually get traders.

## The takeaway

There's no single price tag — there's a model: setup + monthly/revenue-share + operational costs, quoted custom to your scope. Ranges swing widely because "an exchange" varies widely. Price the total, budget for licensing and liquidity, and evaluate everything against how fast it gets you to revenue.
