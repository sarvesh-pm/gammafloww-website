Dubai has become one of the most sought-after homes for crypto businesses, and its **Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA)** is a big reason why: a purpose-built regulator with a clear, category-based licensing framework and strong surrounding infrastructure — banking access, institutional capital, and talent.

## VARA's category-based licences

Rather than one catch-all licence, VARA issues **activity-specific VASP licences**. The main categories are ([VARA](https://www.vara.ae/en/licenses-and-register/licence-applications/), [VARA FAQ](https://www.vara.ae/en/faq/)):

- **Advisory Services** — advice on virtual-asset transactions and portfolios
- **Broker-Dealer Services** — receiving, transmitting, and executing client orders
- **Custody Services** — holding and administering virtual assets
- **Exchange Services** — operating a virtual-asset trading platform

Plus lending/borrowing, management, and transfer/settlement categories. A derivatives exchange centers on the **Exchange Services** licence, typically alongside others depending on the model.

## Why operators like the UAE

- **Regulatory clarity** — a dedicated regulator with published rulebooks, rather than crypto shoehorned into legacy law.
- **Commercial ecosystem** — banking relationships, prime brokerage, and a concentration of crypto-native institutional capital.
- **Speed relative to peers** — a pragmatic, business-aware process (though still a multi-stage one — expect substantive due diligence).

## What to expect in the process

VARA licensing is staged: initial approval, then a full licence granted once governance, capital adequacy, cybersecurity, risk management, and investor-protection expectations are met. Budget for a serious application, external assessments, and qualified local counsel.

## The takeaway

VARA offers one of the clearest routes to a licensed crypto exchange, with category-specific licences and published capital requirements. For a derivatives venue, the Exchange Services licence is the anchor — confirm the current capital and how leveraged products are treated directly with VARA.
