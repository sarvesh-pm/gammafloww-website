The [perpetual future](/blog/what-are-perpetual-futures) — crypto's signature product — is escaping crypto. A fast-growing frontier is **RWA perps**: perpetual contracts on real-world assets like tokenized stocks, forex, and commodities. Here's what they are and why operators are paying attention.

## What RWA perps are

An RWA perp works like a crypto perpetual — no expiry, leverage, a [funding rate](/blog/funding-rates-explained) tethering price to the underlying — but the reference asset is a real-world one. In practice that now includes:

- **Tokenized equities** — perps referencing AAPL, TSLA, MSFT, COIN and others
- **Forex** — major currency pairs
- **Commodities** — notably gold (XAUUSD)

You get exposure to a Tesla or gold price move, 24/7, in crypto rails, with the familiar perp mechanics — without touching the underlying share or bullion.

## Why they're growing

The backdrop is the tokenization boom. The tokenized real-world-asset sector was valued around **$2.08T in 2025 and is projected near $3.01T in 2026** (and $18.74T by 2031) ([Blockonomi](https://blockonomi.com/the-review-of-white-label-crypto-exchanges-market-data-and-trends-2026/)).

As tokenized assets proliferate, perps are a natural way to trade them — extending perpetuals' relevance across both retail and professional circles ([B2Broker](https://b2broker.com/news/institutional-adoption-of-crypto/)).

<Callout title="24/7 access to markets that normally close">
Part of the appeal: traditional stock and commodity markets have hours and holidays. An RWA perp trades continuously — a structural advantage crypto rails offer over the assets they reference.
</Callout>

## The operator angle

For a derivatives venue, RWA perps are a **product-expansion opportunity** — and a set of new challenges:

- **Reliable price feeds.** Referencing off-chain assets makes your [index and oracle](/blog/risk-and-liquidation-engines) design critical; a bad feed on a tokenized stock is a liquidation-cascade risk.
- **Regulatory weight.** A perp on a tokenized *security* can pull you into securities regimes — see the [licensing guide](/blog/crypto-derivatives-licensing-global-guide). This is not the same risk profile as a BTC perp.
- **Same core engine.** The good news: RWA perps reuse your [matching](/blog/inside-a-crypto-matching-engine) and [risk/liquidation](/blog/risk-and-liquidation-engines) stack. If your infrastructure supports perps, adding new underlyings is an extension, not a rebuild.

## The takeaway

RWA perps bring perpetual-futures mechanics to tokenized stocks, forex, and commodities — riding the tokenization boom and offering 24/7 access to markets that normally close. For operators they're a compelling product extension, gated by two things: robust price feeds and the heavier regulation that comes with referencing real-world securities.
